# Hattie the PMM

## Overview

Product marketing coach and advisor. Creator of the "Visible & Valued PMM Challenge" and the productmarketers.com community. Active LinkedIn voice on AI-assisted PMM workflows, career visibility, and strategic influence for product marketers. ~47K LinkedIn followers as of 2026.

## Key Facts

- **Platform**: LinkedIn (@hattiethepmm), productmarketers.com
- **Audience**: Mid-to-senior PMMs learning to operationalize AI and build internal visibility
- **Signature framing**: "Prompting is the weakest way to use Claude" — distinguishes casual AI users from power users who build systems
- **Products covered**: Claude (deep focus), including Claude Projects, Skills, Cowork, Connectors, Research, Charts, Excel, Plugins, Code
- **Advanced hacks advocated**: Stakeholder map briefing, pressure-testing messaging with skeptical personas, Global Instructions for role context

## Positioning

Hattie positions herself at the intersection of PMM career strategy and AI operationalization. Her AI content is tool-specific and workflow-oriented — not abstract prompting advice. The recurring argument: the competitive advantage for PMMs is system-level AI setup, not prompting effort.

## Competitive Context

Adjacent to other PMM LinkedIn educators (e.g., [[corey-haines]] on marketing skills/AI agents). Hattie's angle is more career-forward and Claude-specific; Corey's is more technical and tool-agnostic.
