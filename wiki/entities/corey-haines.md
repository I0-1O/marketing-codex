# Corey Haines

## Overview

Marketer and builder focused on growth, content, and AI-assisted marketing workflows. Creator of the open-source [[ai-agent-skills-for-marketing]] repository for AI coding agents.

## Key Facts

- Creator of [marketingskills](https://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills) — 40+ markdown skill files for AI agents
- Runs **Conversion Factory**, a conversion optimization agency
- Publishes **Swipe Files**, a marketing newsletter
- Author of *Coding for Marketers* (beginner's guide)

## Positioning

Known for making marketing more systematic and AI-accessible. Bridges marketing practice with developer tooling (Claude Code, Cursor, etc.).

## Competitive Context

In the same space as other "prompts for marketers" creators, but differentiated by the structured skill-file pattern and open-source distribution.
