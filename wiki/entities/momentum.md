# Momentum

## Overview

Momentum is an AI-powered sales platform built natively on Salesforce. It automates sales workflow tasks — call summaries, CRM updates, account signals — and publishes an AI prompt library for sales and marketing use cases.

## Key Facts

- Acquired by Salesforce (announced on site as of 2025)
- Focus: revenue teams, sales productivity, deal intelligence
- Prompt library at momentum.io/prompts — curated prompts for sales and GTM workflows
- Category: conversational intelligence / sales AI

## Positioning

Positioned as the AI layer on top of Salesforce that automates the manual work of sales ops — logging, summarizing, updating records. Competes in the revenue intelligence / sales AI space.

## Competitive Context

Plays in the same space as Gong, Chorus (ZoomInfo), Clari, and Salesforce's own Einstein features. Differentiation is tight Salesforce integration and automation-first approach vs. analytics-first competitors.
