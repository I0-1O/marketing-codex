# G2

## Overview

Peer review platform for B2B software. Buyers write structured reviews covering use cases, pros/cons, and comparisons. One of the primary public sources for [[buyer-voice-intelligence]] and competitive research.

## Key Facts

- Largest B2B software review platform by volume
- Reviews are buyer-written, structured, and searchable by category and competitor
- Used by PMMs for: competitive intelligence, buyer vocabulary research, feature perception mapping, and battlecard sourcing

## PMM Use Cases

- **Competitive intelligence** — competitor reviews reveal weaknesses and positioning gaps buyers have already articulated
- **Buyer vocabulary** — reviews use buyer language, not vendor language; useful for messaging and talk track development
- **Feature perception** — star ratings and review text surface where feature perception diverges from reality
- **Win/loss proxy** — reviewers who switched from or to a competitor often explain why

## Limitations

- Reviews skew toward strong opinions (champions and detractors); median buyer underrepresented
- Vendor incentive programs can distort review volume and timing
- Category definitions sometimes lag actual market segmentation
