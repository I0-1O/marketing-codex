# Mailchimp

## Overview

Email marketing and marketing automation platform. Widely cited in marketing circles as a gold standard for brand voice — informal, clear, and human. Their content style guide is open-source (Creative Commons) and has influenced how many B2B companies think about writing guidelines.

## Key Facts

- Published a public content style guide at styleguide.mailchimp.com
- Core voice principle: "We are plainspoken. We strip away hyperbolic language, upsells, and over-promises. We value clarity above all."
- Style guide is open-source and widely referenced across the industry
- Acquired by Intuit in 2021

## Positioning

Known for making deliberate, specific choices about brand voice at a time when most SaaS companies defaulted to corporate-speak. The voice works because it's specific — it doesn't try to sound like everyone.

## Competitive Context

Competes with Klaviyo, HubSpot, Constant Contact, and others in email/marketing automation. Differentiated historically by ease-of-use and brand personality.
