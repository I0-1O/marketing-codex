# Competitor Research Battlecard

A prompt from Momentum's AI prompt library for building competitive research battlecards. Designed for sales teams using Momentum (an AI-powered Salesforce-native sales platform).

## Summary

A six-step structured prompt that walks an AI through generating a competitive battlecard comparing your company against a named competitor. Output covers SWOT analysis for both sides, a persuasive justification for choosing your company, and objection-response pairs.

## The Six-Step Process

1. Request company website URL
2. Request competitor website URL
3. Research both companies' publicly available information (products/services, target markets, positioning, pricing, history, reputation, marketing/branding)
4. Conduct SWOT analysis for both organizations
5. Develop detailed justification for choosing your company over the competitor
6. Identify potential customer objections and provide evidence-backed responses

## Output Structure

- Comprehensive SWOT analysis (both sides)
- Persuasive justification for choosing your company
- Objection-response pairs with supporting evidence

## PMM Notes

This is a sales-use battlecard, not a PMM competitive brief. The prompt is URL-input driven — give it two websites, it does the research. Useful as a lightweight, on-demand alternative to manually maintained battlecard decks. Quality depends entirely on what's publicly available on the websites.

The approach conflates [[battle-card]] (sales tool) with [[competitive-brief]] (PMM research artifact) — they serve different audiences and have different depth requirements.
